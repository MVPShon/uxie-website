Commands

Main

Prefix: u!
Youtube/YT | Brings up a link to a video based on your terms.
MAL | Search for a specific anime's info off of MyAnimeList!
Meme/Edgy | Pulls a random meme off of Reddit!
Dog | Shows a random image of a dog! Aliases (pupper, doge, puppy)
Cat | Shows a random image of a cat! Aliases (kitty, neko)
Roast | Insult your friends with my ever-growing list of roasts and insults!
Copy | Straight forward command. I copy whatever you tell me to. This command works better if I can delete other people's messages.
Dex | Brings up a Pokemon's information and stats!
Ability | Explains a specific Pokemon ability.
Item | Explains a specific Pokemon item
Invite | Provides a link to invite Uxie to your server.
Move | Provides information on a Pokemon move
Roll | Roll the die.
Type | Provides type information for a Pokemon type.
User-info | Provides detailed information about the user mentioned.

Music

Prefix: u!
Add | Adds a song using a specific link to the queue. Usage: u!add https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3WSgJCYIewM
Join | Joins the bot to your music channel. Usage: u!join #MusicChannel
Pause | Pauses the currently playing song.
Play | Plays the songs you've added to the queue.
Queue | Shows the currently queued songs
Resume | Searches for a song by name. You can type u!play to immediately play/add the searched song. Usage: u!search In My Feelings Drake
Skip | Skips the currently playing song.
